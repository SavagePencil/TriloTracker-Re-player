SFX format:

[00100000] is end of sfx marker (dec: 64)

PSG
=======
[NntTvvvv]
[dddddddd] [....dddd]
[...nnnnn]

[01000000] end

SCC
==========
[.WtTvvvv]
[dddddddd] [....dddd]
[...wwwww]

[10000000] end

FM
==========
[.ItTvvvv]
[dddddddd] [..SKooot]
[...iiiii]

[10000000] end
